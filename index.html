Star Guardian

the protectors of light

Who are they?

The Star Guardians are protectors chosen by the First Star to preserve the light in the universe. Accepting the Prime Star's call to protect the world can be overwhelming, but being able to wear the most beautiful outfits in literally the entire universe is a pretty cool perk.

Magical medium!

All the guardians have their magical medium, who help the guardians in all their adventures and journeys

The different mediums are: Kiko, Boki and Baki, Shisa, Multi, Yuuto, radiant staff, Kuro and Shiro, Zephyr, Ina, Umi, Luminus hammer, Pix, Waku, Chiizu, Aru, Value, Sebastien, Dahlia, Saki and Riku among others!

Legend:

When a Star Guardian goes dark, her power revives into new protectors. Star Guardians are chosen for their strength and determination, but above all else, their courage. When several Star Guardians are chosen to serve at the same time, it signals that a cataclysmic battle will ensue somewhere in the universe.

Star Guardians must learn the customs and rituals of a planet in order to blend in with its inhabitants and avoid any suspicion of their true nature. Such activities often help to strengthen the bonds between the younger Star Guardian teams.
